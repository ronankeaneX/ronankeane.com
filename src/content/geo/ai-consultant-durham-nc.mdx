## How to Evaluate AI Companies in Durham

Durham has no shortage of consultants claiming AI expertise. Three questions cut through the noise:

- **Can they handle regulated environments?** Life sciences and healthcare require consultants who understand GxP, HIPAA, and FDA data integrity requirements — not just general AI deployment. Ask for specific examples of compliant implementations.
- **What's their adoption methodology?** Most consultants define success as a working system at go-live. Sustained adoption at 6 months is the real measure. Ask how they specifically drive behavior change after the initial training.
- **Do they build or just advise?** Durham's operational complexity requires hands-on technical execution: n8n automations, API integrations, custom GPTs. Consultants who only advise leave you holding the implementation risk.
